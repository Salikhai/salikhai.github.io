Why IT Ethics is Important

IT ethics is important because technology affects people every day. People who create websites, apps, software, and computer systems should use technology in a responsible way.

IT professionals should protect private information, keep systems secure, and avoid harming users. They should also be honest and fair when they collect data or make decisions using technology.

Examples of IT Ethics

Privacy: Companies should protect user data and not share personal information without permission.

Security: Developers should build secure systems so user information is safe.

Fairness: Data scientists and developers should avoid bias in programs and algorithms.

Honesty: Technology workers should be truthful about how systems work and how data is used.
